How many charges were filed in FY2025?
AI

In FY2025 (October 1, 2024 through September 30, 2025), EEOC received 88,531 charges nationwide. This represents a 3.2% increase over FY2024 (85,779 charges).

The largest contributing bases were:

  • Retaliation — 38,462 charges (43.4%)
  • Disability — 26,559 charges (30.0%)
  • Race — 22,133 charges (25.0%)
  • Sex/Gender — 19,877 charges (22.5%)

Note: charges may allege multiple bases; percentages do not sum to 100.
