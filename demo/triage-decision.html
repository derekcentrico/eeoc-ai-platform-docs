Date: May 21, 2026

Charge No.: 2025-01847

Re: Notice of Right-to-SueDismissal — J. Martinez v. Meridian Logistics Inc.

Dear Mr. Martinez:

The Equal Employment Opportunity Commission has completed its review of the above-referenced charge of employment discrimination. Based on the information provided, the Commission has determined that there is reasonable cause to believe that unlawful employment practices have occurredit is unable to conclude that the information obtained establishes violations of the statutes.

Your charge alleges discrimination based on national origin in violation of Title VII of the Civil Rights Act of 1964. The investigation reviewed records covering the period March 2025 through August 2025.

The Commission's findings indicate a pattern consistent with differential treatment based on national origin in assignment of route territories during the review period. This matter is being referred to the Office of Federal Operations for further consideration.

This notice does not certify that the respondent is in compliance with the statutes. No finding is made as to any other issues that might be construed as having been raised by this charge.

Sincerely,
