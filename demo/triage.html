Charge Narrative
The charging party alleges that despite consistently strong performance evaluations and seniority, they were passed over for a senior engineering promotion in favor of a less experienced colleague. Following an internal discrimination complaint filed with the respondent's HR department on January 12, 2026, the charging party was placed on a performance improvement plan within 30 days.
